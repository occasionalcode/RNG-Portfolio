Hi! I am Riki Tiu

I am a young college boy who majors in Computer Science in Malayan Colleges Mindanao. I delight in listening to and making music, gaming, philosophy, sports and coding. One of my interests in programming is web developing. Though I'm still inexperienced, I plan to work in this field in the long term. I also have a passion for game development given that I'm a gamer, however I do not see myself developing games for a living. In terms of my music-making hobby, I mostly listen to and produce hip-hop songs. Some of my major influences are Playboi Carti, Lil Uzi Vert, Travis Scott, and Lil Mosey. I am also still in the process of branching out to other genres so that I will be a versatile artist.

For my religious beliefs, I am a practicing Christian who gets my core beliefs from the bible. I am interested in Christian apologetics because someday I want to be eloquent in defending my faith through logic, reason, science, and philosophy. 19 years in this world has taught me a lot and made me interested in a lot of things, I am excited and I'm looking forward to see what else life has in store for me.

Dreamer, Wonderer, Competitor

I once dreamt to be a lot of things. I dreamt to be a doctor, to be an NBA player, to be a military officer, to be in the tech industry, and to be a businessman. Sometimes I get confused about what I really want, but I am reminded to take it slow and live one day at a time. I always wonder what the future has in store for me, I also wonder about the past regarding how things came to be from the beginning of time. Being a thinker or an *overthinker* led me to process occurrences that people do not normally think about, maybe it's why I like to play with my mind often. These thought experiments often lead me to extreme ideas which, I think, are the roots of my inconsistent but extreme competitiveness and ambitiousness.

"Dreamer, wonderer, competitor" these are the words I would like to describe the future me. The future "Riki Tiu" that I'm currently striving for day by day by day. I might have setbacks but I'd like to think I'd always comeback, and by the age of 30, I want to embody these three words so that no matter what my final dream would be, I know I will dream big, think big, and win big.
