About me

Music, programming, photography, videography, and sports are some of my favorite things.
I spend most of my leisure time making music and practicing B-roll shots. My first instrument
was the guitar, which I learned when I was six years old. I practiced every day until my small
fingers were able to grasp the tough chords. I composed my first piece of music when I learned the basic
chord progression, and it is about my love for God. To be honest, I'm not a particularly good singer,
but I enjoy singing. I led our church's praise and worship and played the piano whenever it was my turn.
I also sang in the youth and singles choirs, as well as participating in many church-sponsored activities.
Recently, I've been working on a song that will hopefully be released next year.
